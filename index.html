ABOUT

Just tell Sofia how you desire your smart home to behave. She will do the rest. Sofia is your smart home personal assistant. She is able to automatically understand how to program your smart home just asking for few suggestions.

We create the first collaborative smart-home, letting you to speak with her

Our Philosophy

We start from the assumption that it is impossible to interact with a smart home in terms of smart sensors and smart objects. In order to realize the smart home vision, we need to reason about rooms and the activities one wants to do with and within them.
